Katradhu Cinema

Katradhu Cinema is non-profit initiative to create original, refreshing and inspiring stories which aims to capture the essence of World Cinema. With thoughtful, well-crafted, and ethical narratives, team Katradhu Cinema is dedicated to connecting and educating people through the art of cinema. The page was started as an attempt to explore cinema of all categories and explain its intricate meanings and underlying themes, drawing parallels and decoding cinema. We love each and every aspect of Cinema and we love talking about it. Our motive is to "teach cinema, learn cinema". Team KC works to bring each and every interesting detail, review, trivia, anything and everything related to Cinema. We are also expanding into book reviews, historical details, current affairs, job opportunities and other human-interest stories. We want you to be an active part of our journey in exploring cinema. Readers can send their stories and reviews. Selected entries will be uploaded on our website. Go ahead, pen your thoughts and send it to us. Come, lets learn and teach cinema.
